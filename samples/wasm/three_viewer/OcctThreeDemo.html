OCCT + Wasm + Three.js

Cube Minus Cylinder

The solid is generated in Open CASCADE, meshed inside WebAssembly, and handed to Three.js for shaded rendering with explicit topology edges.

Shape A centered cube with a cylindrical through-hole cut along the Z axis.
Faces Triangulated in wasm with per-node normals for proper smooth shading on the hole wall.
Edges Extracted from OCCT topological edges, not inferred from the triangle mesh.
